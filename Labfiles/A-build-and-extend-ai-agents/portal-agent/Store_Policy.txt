ADVENTURE WORKS
CUSTOMER SERVICE & STORE POLICY

Effective Date: January 2026
Version: 2.0

====================
1. RETURNS AND REFUNDS
====================

We want you to be confident in your gear. Most items can be returned within 60 days of purchase.

Process:
1. Start a return in your account at https://returns.adventureworks.com or in any store
2. Items must be unused, with original tags and packaging
3. Bring your order number or receipt
4. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days

Return Windows:
- Standard gear and apparel: 60 days
- Electronics (GPS units, headlamps, watches): 30 days
- Climbing hardware and safety equipment: 30 days, unused only
- Final-sale and clearance items: not returnable

Condition Guidelines:
- Lightly tested footwear may be returned if worn only indoors
- Items showing trail use, dirt, or damage are assessed case by case
- Freeze-dried food and consumables cannot be returned once opened

====================
2. GEAR RENTALS
====================

Rent big-ticket gear before you buy, or for a one-time trip.

How to Rent:
1. Reserve online or in store at least 48 hours before pickup
2. A valid ID and credit card are required at pickup
3. Rental periods are counted in whole days
4. Return gear by 6:00 PM on the due date to avoid late fees

Rental Tiers:
- Standard: day packs, trekking poles, sleeping pads
- Advanced: tents, sleeping bags, camp stoves
- Premium: mountaineering kits, avalanche safety sets, packrafts

Service Levels:
- Standard: pickup in store during business hours
- Priority: reserved and staged for fast pickup
- Express: same-day pickup when stock allows
- Rush: after-hours or emergency pickup by arrangement

Damage and Loss:
- Normal wear is expected and not charged
- Damage beyond normal wear is billed at repair cost
- Lost or unreturned gear is billed at replacement value

====================
3. GUIDED TRIPS AND BOOKINGS
====================

Adventure Works runs guided trips across the world's great ranges.

Booking:
- Reserve a spot online or with a store trip specialist
- A deposit holds your place; the balance is due 14 days before departure
- Group sizes are limited to keep trips safe and personal

Regions We Serve:
- Pacific Northwest
- Rockies
- Patagonia
- Alps
- Andes
- Scandinavia

Cancellations by You:
- 30+ days before departure: full refund of deposit
- 14-29 days before departure: 50% of deposit refunded
- Under 14 days: deposit is non-refundable, balance refunded

Cancellations by Us:
- If we cancel for weather or safety, you receive a full refund or a free reschedule
- Trips may be re-routed for conditions at the guide's discretion

4. SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
====================

Shipping Options:
- Standard (3-5 business days): free on orders over $75
- Express (2 business days): flat rate
- Overnight: available on in-stock items ordered before 1:00 PM

Order Tracking:
- Tracking is emailed when your order ships
- Track any order at https://orders.adventureworks.com
- In-store pickup is usually ready within 2 hours

Delivery Issues:
- Report a lost or damaged package within 14 days of the delivery date
- Keep all packaging until any claim is resolved

====================
5. WARRANTY AND REPAIRS
====================

Manufacturer Warranty:
- Most brands we carry cover defects in materials and workmanship
- Warranty length varies by brand; check the product page or ask a specialist

Adventure Works Trail Guarantee:
- Adventure Works-brand gear is guaranteed against defects for the life of the product
- We will repair, replace, or refund defective Adventure Works-brand items

Repairs:
- We offer in-store repair for zippers, straps, and tent poles
- Send larger repairs to our service center; allow 2-3 weeks
- Repair estimates are provided before any paid work begins

====================

Price Match:
- We match the current price of identical, in-stock items from major competitors

Price Adjustments:
